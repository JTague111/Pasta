How to Make Pasta

Introduction

When I was given this assignment, I decided to write about the first thing that popped into my head and that happend to be how to create delicious home made pasta. It's so easy anyone could do it.

Instructions

  1. Boil water and add salt for taste.
  2. Make the dough.
  3. Roll it into a flat surface.
  4. Put through pasta maker.(if you don't have one, you can use a knife.
  5. Put newly made noodles into water and cook until Al Dente.
